MY FAVOURITE FOODS

STEAK

My favourite food by far, steak is juicy, tender and full of flavour. I especially love a grilled medium-rare steak! This is my main motivation to work the hardest I can to afford daily steak dinners in the future. Cholesterol and heart disease? Never heard of them.

LAMB

Seeing a pattern here? I really like red meats, which brings us to lamb. Some may not like the unique smell and taste mutton and lamb brings, but I love them. Pan-fried lamb chops are complemented perfectly with rosemary and thyme.

COFFEE

I recently grew to love coffee after a particularly stressful yet fun period of my life. It calms, yet it energizes you for the day ahead. Now, I can't imagine how younger me would not like the taste of coffee. Maybe in the future I would think the same of present me, who prefers to dilute the strong flavour with some milk.
